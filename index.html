My Summer - 2020

The summer of 2020 was the quarantine/Covid summer that changed everyone's plans more than we could have imagined. It is not surprising, considering that the last time something like this happened was in 1918.

Our initial quarantine started in mid March while we were on college visits with my son who is a senior in High School. This is the sunset through the windows of the Air B&B we rented in the Hudson Valley, NY.

I spent most of the summer in quarantine, bored, anxious, and cultivating Covid hair and experimenting with new Covid "looks".

Thank fully, Fall is here again!!!
